Logika: Programming Logics
##########################

These notes were adapted from the
`Programming Logics <http://people.cis.ksu.edu/~schmidt/301s14/Lectures/home.html>`__
lecture notes by |Schmidt|.

The main adaptation of the work is to use the Sireum Logika formal
input language as the notations for the propositional, predicate, and programming logics.
